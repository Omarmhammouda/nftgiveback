How do I buy a NFT Giveback?

Once mint is open, you can directly buy an entry through our website or the secondary market via opensea. Be aware of scams and fake links, we will never send you a message first. All announcements and links will be on our discord. With one entry you're eligible to win our 5k a month sweepstakes every month. If you choose not to claim your prize, we do offer an eth exchange instead. Ex: If you win the McLaren and don't want to claim it you are still eligible to 250k in eth. There will be a max of 50 entries per person on the public mint.

What is a whitelist/ presale?

Whitelist is a list of people who get early and guaranteed access to mint at a specific date and window of time. There will be a max of 2 entries per person during presale. Whitelist/presale entries will be 50% off.

How do I get a whitelist role?

We will be hosting random giveaways throughout the weeks, make sure to participate in them to have a chance to win the whitelist role. Another way to enroll in the presale is simply engaging in our community, moderators will be on stand by all day and will give out roles to the most active and helpful users in our discord. We'll also be hosting random trivia games and winners will receive whitelist roles.
